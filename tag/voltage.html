Voltage

 Sun 17 March 2013 by James Knoblauch

Definitions

Voltage (potential difference), v, is a measure of the energy required to move a unit charge through a circuit element, measured in volts (V).

Energy, w, is the capcity to do work, measured in Joules (J)

Mathematical Relationship

For energy w and charge q,

v= dw dq

For points a and b,

v ba=-v ab
